# Airbnb Investment Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

##  Project Overview

This project analyzes Airbnb listing data using Tableau to help a potential investor decide where to buy a property and list it on Airbnb to maximize profit.

The goal is to answer important business questions such as:

- Which locations (zip codes) generate the highest Airbnb prices?
- How does number of bedrooms affect rental price?
- What does the competition look like for different property sizes?
- What are the best times of the year to rent out a property?

The insights from this dashboard can help an investor make data-driven decisions when starting an Airbnb business.

---

#  Business Use Case

Imagine a client who wants to start an Airbnb rental business.

They want to know:

- Where should I buy a property?
- Which locations charge the highest Airbnb prices?
- How many bedrooms should the property have?
- What does the competition look like?
- What are the best times of the year to rent the property?

This Tableau dashboard answers these questions by analyzing price trends, location performance, seasonal demand, and property characteristics.

##  Revenue Trends Throughout the Year

<img width="676" height="424" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/df8a2ad7-8b96-4ecb-8b90-f3565136562f" />


### Description

This line chart shows weekly revenue trends across the year.

It helps determine when Airbnb demand is highest.

### Insight

The data shows:

- Low demand during January and early February
- Higher demand during summer months
- Peak demand during the year-end holiday season (November–December)

### Business Recommendation

If the owner plans to live in the property part-time, they should consider renting the property during:

- Summer vacation months
- Year-end holidays

These periods have higher traveler demand and higher potential revenue.

#  Key Insights from the Dashboard

- Some zip codes generate significantly higher Airbnb prices.
- Location is one of the most important factors when selecting a property.
- Larger properties (5–6 bedrooms) generate higher average prices.
- Competition is highest for small properties and decreases as bedroom count increases.
- Airbnb demand peaks during summer and year-end holidays.

These insights can help investors choose the right property, location, and rental strategy.
